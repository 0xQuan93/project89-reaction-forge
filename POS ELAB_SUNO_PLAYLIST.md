# PoseLab Ambient Curation v1.0
## Suno AI Prompt Library

This document contains a curated list of 12 musical prompts designed to complement the PoseLab studio experience. Each prompt is engineered for Suno AI using high-fidelity keywords consistent with the Project 89 aesthetic.

The PoseLab Ambient Collection
1. "Aurora Mesh"
> Style: Ambient Liquid Electronica, Downtempo, Vapor-glitch
> Keywords: Shimmering pads, crystal-clear transients, 110bpm, aquatic atmosphere, translucent, teal and violet soundscape.
> Prompt: A slow-drifting electronic piece with resonant glass bells, lush aurora-borealis pads, and a subtle, deep sub-bass pulse.
2. "Abyss Scrubber"
> Style: Lo-fi Cyber-jazz, Midnight Lounge
> Keywords: Hissing textures, vinyl crackle, muted trumpet, spatial depth, abyss-black atmosphere, late-night coding.
> Prompt: A chill lo-fi beat with heavily filtered jazz piano, a lazy boom-bap rhythm, and soft rain-on-glass textures.
3. "Glassmorphism"
> Style: Minimalist IDM, Glitch-Ambient
> Keywords: Percussive clicks, liquid textures, high-fidelity, transparent layers, icy, 90bpm.
> Prompt: High-definition micro-percussion over warm, evolving analog pads. Sounds like light reflecting off frosted glass.
4. "Signal Green"
> Style: Synthwave, Retro-Future, Dreamy
> Keywords: 80s analog synth, arpeggiated sequences, bright, optimistic, signal-green neon, 120bpm.
> Prompt: A driving but gentle synthwave track with shimmering leads, retro drum machines, and a sense of forward momentum through a digital city.
5. "Pose Logic"
> Style: Math-Rock Ambient, Clean Electric Guitar
> Keywords: Math-rock, intricate tapping, clean chorus guitar, rhythmic, polyrhythmic, precise, structural.
> Prompt: Intricate, interlocking clean guitar melodies with a math-rock influence. Technical but soothing, perfect for focused creative work.
6. "Glitch-Tab"
> Style: Experimental Ambient, Glitch, Foley
> Keywords: UI sounds, mouse clicks, keyboard clacks, digital artifacts, rhythmic glitch, 100bpm.
> Prompt: A rhythmic ambient piece constructed entirely from digital UI sounds, soft glitches, and rhythmic white noise.
7. "Vapor Scape"
> Style: Vaporwave, Slushwave, Hyper-reverb
> Keywords: Slowed + Reverb, nostalgic, 80s mall music, ethereal, dreamy, pink and cyan hues.
> Prompt: Heavily processed, slowed-down 80s pop melodies drowning in reverb and delay. A hazy, nostalgic dreamscape.
8. "Spatial Scenery"
> Style: Cinematic Post-Rock, Ambient
> Keywords: Swelling guitars, cinematic, wide soundstage, epic but quiet, emotional, delay pedals.
> Prompt: A slow-building post-rock track with atmospheric guitar swells, distant piano notes, and a deep sense of open space.
9. "Neon Drip"
> Style: Future Bass Ambient, Kawaii-Lo-fi
> Keywords: Bubbling synths, liquid drips, cute, bright, bubbly, 140bpm (half-time).
> Prompt: A bubbly, high-energy but quiet track featuring "drip" sound effects, bright pluck synths, and a very soft future-bass rhythm.
10. "Solar Flare"
> Style: Deep House, Melodic Techno (Ambient)
> Keywords: Warm, orange, solar, pulsing, organic, 124bpm, hypnotic.
> Prompt: A hypnotic melodic house track with warm analog stabs, a steady organic kick drum, and solar-flare-inspired white noise sweeps.
11. "Pixelate"
> Style: Chiptune-Ambient, 8-Bit Lo-fi
> Keywords: Bit-crushed, nostalgic, game-boy, lo-fi, square waves, soft.
> Prompt: A melancholic chiptune melody played with soft square waves, light bit-crush distortion, and a simple lo-fi drum loop.
12. "Final Render"
> Style: Zen-Ambient, New Age, Drone
> Keywords: Singing bowls, long drones, peaceful, static, high-frequency, zen.
> Prompt: A pure ambient drone track with evolving harmonic frequencies and soft singing bowls. Zero percussion, total immersion.
